The Steamwrought Tomb

Unique and custom boss fight versus the Scalding Sentinel. 

Boss fight with 7+ custom abilities, a custom model, VFX, SFX, and weapon!

How to Play:

Create the Steamwrought Tomb portal shard in the Arcanist's Workbench.
Enter the custom instance The Steamwrought Tomb via the Ancient Gateway by interacting with it with the Steamwrought Tomb portal shard in hand.
Gear up with the chests inside of the instance!
Either go through the dungeon straight ahead or teleport straight to the boss with a portal right behind spawn.

Recommended for at least 2 players!
